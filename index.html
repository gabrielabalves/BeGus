Begu is a dynamic graph rendering system which completely depicts your current Architecture on the fly while you build your system. It allows you to make educated decisions at any time by having the complete overview of the current system state and streamline your IT with business future needs, by analyzing, optimizing, and organizing all assets on the go.

A tool that grows with your system

Your overview grows with your development project

Begu builds a snapshot after each code change of the current Architecture of your System and presents it in a digestible manner by showing the interconnectivity your system comprises of. This helps you to see at a glance how your system is interconnected.

A tool that easily integrates into your daily development lifecycle workflow

We work alongside your natural development cycle, connecting to your favorite version control system and processing your code directly from there.

Connect your system using OAuth2.0, confirm your code, continue your development while we verify all changes made to your system and update changes in real time.

The end result is easy digestible blocks of information to work with.

Always know your project and development history over time

The graph saves snapshots of the System's state over time so you can easily track and analyze the development of your project. All information is being saved and can be looked at later to make educated decisions over future development of the system.

Create Logical Sections of your System which fit your Business IT Requirements

1

Categorize your System

Build Sections

The Graph allows you to summarize several parts of your system and create a Section out of it. This helps you to make better sense out of an ever growing system with ever growing functionality.

2

Assign Custom Meaning

Add Tags

Add Tags to your Sections which help you to connect specific parts of your code to specific meaningful words. It also helps you to filter more efficiently for parts of your system.

3

Know who works in your System

Assign Tasks

Assign Jira Work Tasks to your Sections. Thanks to this, you know who works on what part of your system and the status of it.

4

Documentation where it is needed

Documentation directly to tasks

Integrate documentation at the spots in the system where it makes sense. Make a direct connection between the documentation of your work and the parts of the system where it is being developed on.
